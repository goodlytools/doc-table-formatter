Contact

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

Common questions

  • How do I open the sidebar? Open any Google Doc, then click Extensions → Doc Table Formatter → Open sidebar.
  • Does the add-on store any data? No. It runs entirely inside the document you have open and does not transmit data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy.
  • What permissions does it need? Only access to the current document so it can format tables you select.

Reporting an issue

When emailing support, please include:

  • A description of what you tried and what happened
  • A screenshot, if possible
  • Your browser and operating system
